# Context Fragmentation Is Gradual

## Observation

AI-assisted workflows rarely lose continuity all at once. They usually degrade
through small gaps between intent, current state, review status, and the next
action.

## Pattern

When those pieces drift apart, each new session spends more time rebuilding the
operational picture than moving the work forward.

## Principle

A useful workflow should keep intent, state, constraints, and next action close
enough that the work can be resumed without rediscovering the whole context.
